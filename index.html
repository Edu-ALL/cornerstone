Welcome to THE CORNERSTONE

The Future Shaped Without Students, Will Not Work for Students.

The Cornerstone is a student-centered conference built around a series of panel discussions that bring together students, educators, policymakers, and leaders to reflect on the future of education in the age of artificial intelligence. The conference creates a space where student voices meet experience and leadership to discuss how AI is shaping education, global order, creative industry, and environmental responsibility.

Held in the spirit of Hari Pendidikan Nasional and marking EduALL’s 10-year journey, The Cornerstone reflects EduALL’s commitment to educational impact and inclusion. In collaboration with Indonesia Mengajar, the conference also serves as a fundraising platform to support access to quality education in remote and underserved regions across Indonesia.

What Awaits AT THE CORNERSTONE

1     Thought-Provoking Panel Discussions

Engage with leading voices from education, technology, and policy as they explore how artificial intelligence is shaping global order, creative industry, education systems, and environmental responsibility. Each discussion is introduced by student perspectives, bringing the voices of the next generation into the conversation.

2     Meaningful Conversations and Networking

Beyond the stage, curated mingle sessions create opportunities for participants to exchange perspectives and ideas with students, educators, professionals, and leaders across disciplines.

3     A Conference with a Shared Impact

The Cornerstone is also part of a fundraising initiative in collaboration with Indonesia Mengajar. A portion of every ticket purchased will contribute to supporting educational initiatives in underserved regions across Indonesia, while audience votes during the panels also contribute additional donations.
