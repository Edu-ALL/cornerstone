# 🏛️ THE CORNERSTONE

---

## 📛 Project Name

**THE CORNERSTONE Conference 2026**

A student-centered AI conference bringing together students, educators, policymakers, and leaders in a cross-generational dialogue on the future of education in the age of artificial intelligence.

---

## 🎯 Goal

✅ **Think Deeper For Indonesia's Future**
- ✅ Create a space where student voices meet experience and leadership
- ✅ Discuss how AI is shaping education, global order, creative industry, and environmental responsibility
- ✅ Celebrate Hari Pendidikan Nasional & EduALL's 10-year journey
- ✅ Fundraising platform to support quality education in remote regions across Indonesia in collaboration with Indonesia Mengajar
- ✅ Build inclusive future policies that actually work for students

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📅 Event Details
- 🗓️ **Date**: May 9, 2026
- 📍 **Venue**: Swasana Lippo Kuningan Grand Ballroom
- 🤝 **Organizer**: EduALL x Indonesia Mengajar

- 👥 **Cross Generational Dialogue** - Students, Educators, Policymakers and Industry Leaders together

  - 🎨 Creative Industry
  - 🌱 Environmental Responsibility

## 🚀 About

The future shaped without students, will not work for students.

The Cornerstone is held in the spirit of National Education Day and marks EduALL's 10-year commitment to educational impact and inclusion. Every ticket purchased also contributes to supporting educational initiatives in underserved regions through Indonesia Mengajar.
